Issues with interfaces.

No braille for the visually impaired on the outside interface.

No indication of tje current floor from the inside of the elevator.

The "Alarm" and "Stop" buttons are both too close to other buttons and could be pressed accidentally.

No braille for the "Open Door", "Alarm", and "Stop" button.

Some common things used on elevators

A button on the outside of the elevator to indicate if you want to call the elevator to go up or down.

A button to close and open the doors.

Buttons to declare what floor you want to go to.

A button to stop the elevator.

An button to call for help incase of emergencies.

A vidual indicator of what floor the elevator is currently on from both the outside and inside.

A railing to hold onto in the elavator.

The current interface is simple and enough for only two floors, but can still be improved upon
